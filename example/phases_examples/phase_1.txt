Game title: Shape Sprint (geometry dash)

Rules: Player chooses a different shape, circle, or square.
 Player will then be put into one of 3 maps
  The player can choose between the easy, medium and hard difficulty maps
The player can not touch any obstacles, or they will die
Get to the end of all 3 maps to win a surprise! (Peter Griffin's face saying “You won!)

Mechanics: 
Jump
Auto move
Hitboxes with spikes 
Collision with platforms
End goal at the end of the level
